# Project Name: **ReLoop**


**Buy Smart. Sell Easy. Reuse Better.**

---

## 1. Problem Statement

Buying second-hand or refurbished products online is risky due to:

* Lack of trust in sellers
* No standard way to judge product condition
* Unclear pricing for used items
* Poor user experience on existing platforms

On the other hand, sellers struggle to:

* Price their used products correctly
* Find genuine buyers quickly
* Prove product quality

---

## 2. Solution Overview

**ReLoop** is a smart re-commerce (re-buy) platform that allows users to **buy and sell verified pre-owned products** with a strong focus on **trust, transparency, and sustainability**.

It combines:

* Modern e-commerce UX
* Condition-based product scoring
* Smart pricing insights
* Sustainability impact awareness

---

## 3. Target Audience

* College students
* Budget-conscious buyers
* First-job professionals
* Eco-conscious consumers
* Gadget upgraders

---

## 4. Core Features

### Buyer Side

* Browse refurbished / pre-owned products
* Filter by condition (A / B / C)
* Compare refurbished vs new price
* View seller trust score
* Wishlist & dark mode support

### Seller Side

* Simple product listing flow
* Condition selection & issue disclosure
* Auto-suggested resale price
* Demand score estimation
* Seller dashboard

### Trust & Quality Layer

* Product condition score
* Seller reputation score
* Platform-verified badge (optional)
* Return eligibility indicator
